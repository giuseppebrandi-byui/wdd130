About us

How it all started

In spite of the fact that our story is not unique, it is the foundation for what we have become and what we have achieved. Initially, a group of university students met weekly at Leeds' local meet-up for developers. There were many talented people with great ideas, but little practical experience in designing and building websites. A few months later, the meet-up organizer decided to close the weekly gathering after receiving a new job offer far from home. As a result, I and a small group of developers decided to continue our journey. We worked on small projects, and then we decided to help a few local businesses get online. The experience was valuable, and we learned what works and what doesn't in the virtual world. That's how we established the Website Consultant Buddy.

Our Mission

We specialise in designing websites for small to medium sized businesses across the UK. We put the personal touch into the internet, and we love meeting face to face with our clients. By finding out as much as possible about your business, your competitors, your sector and your goals for the future, our professional designers are able to create visual representations of how your website could look and feel. You are under no obligation whatsoever at this stage, as we believe in putting our money where our mouth is. We will recommend the functions your website will need to ensure it delivers all your expectations and will talk you through everything in a way that make sense to you and your business. We believe in giving every Website Consultant Buddy client more for their money and that's why every WCB website is fitted as standard with many functions that other web design businesses would charge extra for. So, if you are looking for a search engine friendly website - one that gives you an edge over competitors, grow your business, achieve your online goals, and exploit the commercial potential of the Internet, then a WCB website is the answer.
